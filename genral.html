General Vehicle Servicing

Our general vehicle servicing covers a wide range of routine maintenance and minor repairs to ensure your vehicle runs smoothly. Below are the key services we provide under general servicing:

Engine Diagnostics

1500 to as per condition

Our technicians use advanced diagnostic tools to check your engine's performance. This helps identify any potential issues before they become major problems, ensuring your vehicle runs efficiently.

Fluid Checks and Refills

500 to 800

We check all essential vehicle fluids, including engine oil, transmission fluid, brake fluid, power steering fluid, and coolant. If necessary, we top them up or replace them to maintain optimal performance.

Filter Replacements

250 to 400

We replace air filters, cabin filters, and fuel filters as part of our general servicing. Clean filters improve your vehicle’s fuel efficiency and air quality inside the cabin.

Battery Health Check

100 to 800

We inspect your vehicle’s battery and charging system to ensure your battery is working properly. This can help prevent unexpected breakdowns due to battery failure.

Suspension Check

500 to 1000

Our technicians inspect your vehicle's suspension system, including shocks, struts, and springs, to ensure a smooth and safe ride. If necessary, we can replace any worn components.

Exhaust System Inspection

1000 to 5000

The exhaust system is vital for reducing emissions and noise. We check for leaks, rust, and signs of wear and replace components as needed to ensure your vehicle meets environmental standards.

Wiper Blades Replacement

2000 to asper condition

Wiper blades are essential for clear visibility in all weather conditions. We inspect and replace wiper blades if they show signs of wear, ensuring a safe driving experience in any weather.

Lights and Indicators Check

50 to 500

We check the condition and functionality of all vehicle lights, including headlights, brake lights, turn signals, and interior lights, to ensure proper visibility and safety on the road.
